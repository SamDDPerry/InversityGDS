Tips for New Drivers

  • Practice regularly: Try to practice in different conditions and environments, quiet roads, busy streets, and various weather conditions.
  • Learn the Highway Code: Thoroughly understand road signs, rules, and regulations before taking your theory test.
  • Insurance considerations: Research different insurance options and consider black box insurance for potentially lower premiums.
  • Maintenance awareness: Learn basic vehicle maintenance like checking tire pressure, oil levels, and understanding warning lights.
  • Confidence building: Start with shorter journeys and gradually build up to longer trips and more challenging routes.
  • Safety first: Always maintain safe distances, check mirrors regularly, and avoid distractions while driving.
  • P plates: Consider using P plates after passing your test to let other drivers know you're a new driver.
